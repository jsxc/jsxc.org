Ilias Integration

Ilias provides a complete e-learning experience. JSXC allows students and teachers to interact with each other, even when active in another application or with a native client.

One of the uses is multi-user chat among students and/or teachers of a class. Basic support for the most common MUC rooms was introduced with JSXC 2.0.0 and will be supporting more room options and configuring them in the near future.

The standard features of JSXC are extended by:

  • Unified login: Logging into Ilias also logs you in to JSXC/XMPP
